Privacy Policy

How we protect your information and respect your privacy (spoiler: we take it seriously).

Last updated: August 2025

At Sandy's Snacks, we believe privacy should be simple and transparent. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We're real people running a real service, not a corporate data harvesting operation.

The Short Version

We collect the minimum information needed to run our snack subscription service. We don't sell your data, we don't spam you, and we don't do anything weird with your information. We're here to provide great snacks, not to invade your privacy.
